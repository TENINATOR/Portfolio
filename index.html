About Me

I'm a passionate technical artist and programmer with experience in game development, robotics, and 3D work. My journey includes participating in Ubisoft Next 2024, winning 3rd place at the ENCANTUS OTU Case Competition, joining Ontario tech University's competitive Robotics team "Robomaster" as a Programmer, and being one of the representatives for my University at CAN-WiC 2024. I've worked on projects like a Flashcard app, Game making (In the Technical art, 3d modelling and Programming disciplines), and robotics experiments. I thrive at the intersection of tech and creativity which shows in my work :].
